This page is about light

Light, the radiant energy that illuminates our world, is a form of electromagnetic radiation that allows us to perceive the beauty and complexity of our surroundings. From the warm glow of the sun to the intricate patterns of a laser beam, light exists in a spectrum of wavelengths, each carrying different amounts of energy and contributing to the vibrant tapestry of colors we see. It travels in waves and also behaves as particles, a duality that has fascinated scientists for centuries and continues to unlock the secrets of the universe.
